About The Gathering of Midwives

The Gathering of Midwives (TGM), is a non-denominational spiritual meeting where men and women around the world would come to host the presence of God and receive what they need for their priesthood in their respective territories (marriages and families).

Our mission is to empower end-time saints to serve as watchmen, priests, and gatekeepers, ensuring families align with the original template of Christ and the Church.

  • - Desire to wait on God and have Him minister to you
  • - Desire to hear God clearly and know His plans for your family.
  • - Desire to join men and women who will stand as Priests, Gatekeepers, and Watchmen over their households.

We believe that God has a perfect plan and purpose for every family, and that He wants to bless and restore them through His love and grace. We believe that families are the building blocks of society, and that when they are aligned and nurtured according to God’s design, they can transform the world for His glory.
